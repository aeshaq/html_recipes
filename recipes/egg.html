Scrambled Eggs

Description

The perfect egg recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • A tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs in a bowl
  2. Add some salt
  3. Warm your pan under medium heat
  4. Add olive oil
  5. When the pan is slightly smoking add the eggs
  6. Keep moving the egss around
  7. Add a dash of pepper
  8. Cook until golden
  9. Enjoy!
