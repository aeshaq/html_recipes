Chicken Breast

Description

The perfect Chicken breast recipe

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken breasts
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Whatever other spices you enjoy!

Steps

  1. Make sure the chicken is defrosted
  2. Heat some butter on the stove and paint the chicken with it
  3. Sprinkle your salt and spices over the chicken. Make sure to get both sides
  4. Heat your oven to 450 F
  5. Place chicken in oven for 15-20 minutes flipping once
  6. Enjoy!
